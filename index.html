Music Production

High-End Recording

Offering world-class recording studios with advanced audio equipment, enabling artists to capture their sound in the highest fidelity possible. Sessions are tailored to the artist's needs, whether they are recording a single, an EP, or a full album.

Custom Mixing & Mastering

Providing expert mixing and mastering to polish the raw recordings. Engineers work closely with artists to craft the perfect balance of instruments, vocals, and effects, ensuring that each track meets industry standards for audio quality.

Remote Sessions

Utilizing virtual collaboration tools, EchoWave Studios allows artists from around the world to work with its engineers remotely, making it easier for global talent to access high-end production services.

Artist Development

Branding & Image Building

Helping artists develop their brand, from logos and album covers to social media presence. EchoWave Studios partners with graphic designers and social media experts to create a cohesive visual identity that matches the artist's music.

Media Coaching

Artists can benefit from training in public speaking, interviews, and media appearances. This service includes advice on engaging with fans and building rapport with audiences during performances or press events.

Music Distribution

Digital Distribution

Assisting artists in getting their music on major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and YouTube Music. This service includes metadata optimization and playlist pitching to ensure that the music reaches the right audience.

Licensing & Sync Deals

Facilitating licensing opportunities for artists to place their music in movies, TV shows, commercials, and video games. EchoWave works with a network of sync agencies to help artists gain exposure and revenue from these placements.

Physical Media

For artists who want to release their work in physical formats, EchoWave provides services to produce vinyl, CDs, and even limited-edition merchandise to accompany releases.
